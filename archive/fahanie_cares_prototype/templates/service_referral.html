Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service referral system.

How long does the referral process take?

Processing times vary depending on the service requested and urgency level. Emergency requests are processed within 24-48 hours, while standard requests typically take 5-10 business days.

What documents do I need to provide?

Required documents depend on the specific service requested. Common documents include valid ID, proof of residence, income verification, and any supporting documentation related to your specific case.

How will I be updated about my referral status?

You'll receive updates via your preferred communication method (email or SMS). You can also check your referral status anytime by logging into your account and entering your reference number.

Can I submit multiple referral requests?

Yes, you can submit multiple requests for different services. However, we recommend focusing on your most urgent needs first to ensure efficient processing.

What if my request is denied?

If your referral cannot be processed, our team will provide a detailed explanation and suggest alternative services or options that may better suit your needs.

Is there a fee for using the referral service?

No, the #FahanieCares service referral system is completely free. There are no charges for submitting requests or receiving assistance from our team.
