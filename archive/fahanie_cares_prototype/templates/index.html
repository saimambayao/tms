Latest Updates

Stay informed about the latest activities, programs, and parliamentary updates from MP Uy-Oyod.

Community Outreach Program in Maguindanao

MP Uy-Oyod's team successfully conducted a comprehensive community outreach program in various municipalities of Maguindanao, providing essential services and aid to over 1,500 constituents.

New Bill on Youth Empowerment Filed

MP Uy-Oyod has officially filed a new bill aimed at empowering the youth through enhanced educational opportunities and entrepreneurship support. The bill seeks to establish dedicated funding and mentorship programs.

Partnership with Local NGOs for Disaster Relief

#FahanieCares has forged a strategic partnership with several local non-governmental organizations to streamline disaster relief efforts across the region. This collaboration aims to provide faster and more efficient aid during emergencies.
