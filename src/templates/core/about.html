Our Mission

#FahanieCares is MP Atty. Sittie Fahanie S. Uy-Oyod's flagship constituent service initiative dedicated to bringing essential government services directly to the people. Our mission is to create a more accessible, responsive, and effective government that truly serves the needs of every Bangsamoro.

Through innovative digital tools, community engagement, and dedicated service, we aim to bridge the gap between government resources and the people who need them most.

Serving the Bangsamoro with dedication and excellence

About MP Sittie Fahanie S. Uy-Oyod

MP Atty. Sittie Fahanie S. Uy-Oyod is a dedicated public servant committed to improving the lives of her constituents through effective legislation and direct service delivery. With a background in law, she brings a unique perspective to solving community problems and advocating for positive change.

Her legislative priorities include education, healthcare access, social services, and economic empowerment. Through the #FahanieCares initiative, she extends her office's reach directly into communities to ensure government services are accessible to all.

MP Uy-Oyod believes that representation means being present and responsive to constituent needs, which is why she has established this digital platform to complement her field outreach efforts.

Committed to legislative excellence and constituent service
