Our Mission

Serbisyong Bangsamoro is MP Amiroddin Gayak's flagship constituent service initiative dedicated to bringing essential government services directly to the people. Our mission is to serve the Bangsamoro people with utmost integrity and excellence and help them access balanced, quality Islamic and English education.
